steps:

  - "Toss pepper, zucchini, onion and chickpeas with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt and pepper."
  - "Spread on the tray in a single layer and roast for 20–25 minutes until caramelised."
  - "Mix yoghurt with lemon juice and a pinch of salt to make the sauce."
  - "Warm the wraps in a dry pan for 30 seconds each side."
  - "Spread hummus on each wrap, pile on the roasted veggies, drizzle with yoghurt sauce and top with rocket."

These wraps are proof that a quick lunch can still feel really satisfying. The smoked paprika is the secret — it gives everything a lovely depth without any effort.
