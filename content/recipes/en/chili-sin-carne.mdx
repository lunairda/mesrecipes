steps:
  - "Heat the olive oil in a large wide pan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened and translucent."
  - "Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Add the smoked paprika, cumin, chilli powder, coriander and turmeric, and stir for 30 seconds to toast the spices."
  - "Add the diced bell peppers and butternut squash. Stir to coat in the spices and cook for 3–4 minutes."
  - "Pour in the canned tomatoes, then fill the empty can halfway with water and add that too. Season generously with salt and pepper."
  - "Add the kidney beans, chickpeas and corn. Stir everything together, bring to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and simmer uncovered for 20 minutes, until the squash is tender and the sauce has thickened."
  - "Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve topped with sliced radishes for a fresh, peppery crunch."
