I spent two weeks in South Korea and had a yoghurt smoothie almost every single day. They were thick, refreshing and just sweet enough — the kind of thing you don't think about until you're back home and suddenly craving it at 8am.

This is my version: lighter, simpler, and made with whatever frozen fruit I have in the freezer. Blueberries are my favourite base but this works beautifully with frozen mango, strawberries or mixed berries too. The lemon juice is a small thing that makes a big difference — it just brightens everything up.
