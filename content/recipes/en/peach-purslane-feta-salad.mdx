This salad started as a side for my baked lentil patties and quickly became something I make on its own too. The purslane has a lovely juicy crunch, the peaches bring sweetness, and the moutarde à l'ancienne gives the dressing just enough bite to pull everything together.

The yoghurt dressing is very forgiving — add more lemon if you like it tangier, or a tiny drizzle of honey if your peaches aren't quite sweet enough.
